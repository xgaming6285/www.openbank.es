What do you get with the Open Payroll Account?

Arrange for your salary to be paid into Openbank1 and enjoy all these advantages:

  • You will receive 0.5% cashback on your main bills: electricity, gas, telephone, mobile phone and Internet.
  • Free Open Credit Card.
  • The option to receive an advance on your salary with Openbank's Overdraft Protection.
  • You will earn a better interest rate with the fee-free ​​​​​​​Openbank Savings Account.

1 You can enjoy the benefits of the Payroll Account if: (i) you arrange for your salary, pension or unemployment benefit of at least €900 to be paid directly into your account; or (ii) you receive monthly transfers of at least €900 from any other bank.

How can you enjoy the advantages of the Open Payroll Account?

By having your salary, pension or unemployment benefits of at least €900 paid directly into your account. You can also take advantage of the benefits of the Open Payroll Account when you receive regular transfers (from any bank) of at least €900.

What if you don't receive a salary, pension or unemployment benefits?

You can still open the account. All you have to do is make one monthly transfer of at least €900 to be eligible for the benefits.

What can you do for free?

  • ​Obtain or renew the Open Debit Card for the main holder.
  • Obtain or renew the Open Credit Card for all holders.
  • Deposit or withdraw cash in euros with the debit card at any Banco Santander branch or ATM in Spain.
  • Withdraw cash with the debit card from over 6,000 Banco Santander ATMs in Spain.
  • Make or receive domestic transfers in euros within Spain and international within the EU.
  • Pay all of your bills by direct debit.

How can you arrange for your salary to be paid in directly? And your bills direct debited?

It's simple:

Salary: Fill in this application, sign it and give it to your company. You can also give them your Open Payroll Account Number.

Bills: You have several ways to arrange direct debits for your bills:

  • You can upload an image of your bill. In your Customer Area, go to “Accounts/Direct Debits”, click on “Direct Debit Management” and then “Pay bills by direct debit”.
  • Or, if you prefer, fill in this application form and upload it to your Customer Area by clicking on Documentation > More options > Upload documentation. Please select "Direct debits" from the list to upload the documents.
  • You can also send the application by post, with a photocopy of your bills, to Openbank, Paseo de la Castellana 134, 28046 Madrid (Spain).​​​​​​​

How can you open an Open Payroll Account?

If you are a customer:

You can switch your Current Account to a Payroll Account, without changing account numbers.

Enter the customer area and select the Current Account where you already have or are going to deposit your income and click on the "Open Payroll Account" option at the bottom right-hand side of the screen.

If you are not yet a customer:

  • Call us on +34 91 177 33 37
  • Come see us at our main branch at Paseo de la Castellana, 134, Madrid.
