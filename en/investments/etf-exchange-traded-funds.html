What are ETFs or listed funds?

They are investment funds that are traded on the stock market. Unlike traditional funds, whose shares are subscribed to or redeemed at their net asset value (calculated following the closing of each trading session), units of ETFs are bought and sold on a secondary market, like shares or bonds.

How can I invest in ETFs?

Issue your purchase order with us through our website or by phone. ETFs have the same trading hours and types of orders as shares. They are executed online, so you are able to see the transaction reflected in your account immediately.
